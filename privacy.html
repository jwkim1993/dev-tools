Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Introduction

JSON Prettier ("we," "our," or "the tool") is a free online JSON formatting and validation tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

The most important thing to know: all JSON processing happens entirely in your browser. Your JSON data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. There is no backend server that receives or stores your input.

Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • JSON input data — Any JSON you paste, upload, or load into the tool is processed entirely within your browser using JavaScript. It is never sent to any server.
  • Formatting preferences — Your indentation settings, sort key preferences, and other tool options are handled in your browser's memory and are not tracked or stored remotely.
  • User accounts — We do not require or offer user accounts. There is no registration, login, or profile system.
  • Personal information — We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit JSON Prettier, our hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages) may automatically collect certain standard web server log information, including:

  • IP address (used for security and abuse prevention)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Date and time of access

This information is collected by Cloudflare as part of their standard hosting infrastructure and is subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy. We do not have direct access to individual-level log data.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Essential Cookies

Cloudflare may set essential cookies for security purposes (such as bot detection and DDoS protection). These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled.

Third-Party Advertising Cookies

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings or by visiting www.aboutads.info.

For more information about how Google uses data from sites that use their services, please visit Google's Partner Sites Policy.

Third-Party Services

JSON Prettier uses the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare Pages — Website hosting and content delivery. Subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense (if applicable) — Advertising. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

Since your JSON data is processed entirely in your browser and is never transmitted to our servers, the primary security consideration is your own device and browser. We recommend:

  • Keeping your browser up to date
  • Being cautious when loading JSON from untrusted URLs
  • Clearing your browser's clipboard after copying sensitive data

The website is served over HTTPS, ensuring that the tool's code is delivered securely to your browser.

Children's Privacy

JSON Prettier is a general-purpose developer tool and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access information we hold about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of personalized advertising
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Since we do not collect personal data beyond standard server logs (managed by Cloudflare), most of these rights are addressed by our minimal data collection practices. For advertising opt-out, please use the links provided in the Cookies section above.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the contact channels available on our About page.
